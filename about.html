About The
Region

The Ozarks

The Ozark Region has an expansion history going back thousands of years. In the early days, Paleoindians lived in the area until Europeans began to settle here in the 1800s.

America's First National River

In 1972, the Buffalo National River became America's first national river. It flows for 135 miles through the Ozark mountains and is one of the last few undammed rivers in the country.
